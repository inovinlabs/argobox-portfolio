How I'm Running Services

Here's a sample of how I deploy and manage services in my infrastructure:

# K3s deployment for Gitea with persistent storage
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: gitea
  namespace: argobox
spec:
  replicas: 1
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: gitea
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: gitea
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: gitea
        image: gitea/gitea:latest
        ports:
        - containerPort: 3000
        volumeMounts:
        - name: gitea-data
          mountPath: /data
